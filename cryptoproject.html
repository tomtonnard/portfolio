Transaction App

research - usability testing/prototyping -
UX/UI design

This project is currently beeing realised for a new blockchain.
The name of the project won't be disclosed due to an NDA.

I've developed a high fidelity mobile web-application that allows its users to make transactions on a new blockchain.

Discovery
Phase

A company is investing money into the development of apps that will run on their new blockchain.

They want a web-application that will let users make transactions to eachother.

1.

The big question

How do I create a user-friendly application that allows transactions of different crypto currency on a new blockchain?

2.

Competator research

To realise an application that handles transactions of currency, you won't have to reinvent the wheel.

Looking at transaction apps in other sectors gave great insights in the flow and design of the application.

3.

Ideation

With the info aquired by the competator research and interviews with the investor, sketching and wireframing commenced to establish the main functionalities of the app.

4.

Prototyping the product

Prototypes were created and tested with the users from various target groups. Multiple rounds led to multiple iterations and refinement.

Evolution

Countless rounds of prototypes, user testing, and tech exploration helped to refine the finalised interface and product.
